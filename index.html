My Professional Journey

I am an Engineering Analyst at Google, a technical mentor, and the founder of One Way Education. An alumna of BITS Pilani (Goa Campus), my work centers on bridging the gap between sophisticated data analytics and real-world human problem-solving.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of helping business analysts and product managers scale their skills, mentoring over 1,000 career-transitioning students across programs like Masai School, and collaborating on systems automation globally. I believe technology is at its best when it solves a specific problem for a specific person.

My journey in tech is driven by curiosity, analytical logic, and a passion for teaching. I graduated with a dual degree from BITS Pilani, Goa Campus, where I balanced technical rigors with leading impact initiatives like 180 Degrees Consulting. My career took off in the fast-paced fintech space at Groww as a Business Analyst, where I designed complex database queries, conducted deep-dive root cause analyses, and drove end-to-end statistical hypothesis tests.

Today, as an Engineering Analyst at Google, I leverage analytical workflows to solve problems at scale. Beyond my corporate role, I am deeply committed to education. Through One Way Education, I design learning programs that turn complex data, statistics, and machine learning models into accessible, career-ready skills.

I regularly partner with premier academic institutions like IIT Patna, BITS Goa, and BITSoM to mentor future tech leaders. Whether orchestrating international workflow automation or troubleshooting an SQL script, my focus remains the same: using technology to empower people and make systems more human.
